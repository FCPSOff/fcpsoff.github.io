FCPSOff Online

Our aim is to create a free and privacy respecting digital enviorment for students attending
FCPS schools. We acomplish  this through close collaboration with the districts IT and
Cybersecurity teams. We also try to take part in the identification and reporting of security
vulnerabilities in FCPS digital infrastructure in order to improve the overall online experience
at FCPS.


If you wish to join our mission or contribute to one of our projects you can email us
at anonstudent1432@protonmail.com or send a PR to one of our projects on github.
